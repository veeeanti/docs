# What we use for our games

You may have noticed by now that we often use "gbe_fork" in our uploads, this is a steam emu - and arguably one of the better ones out there. It is a fork of Goldberg Steam Emu maintained by detanup01 on Github, this is the most up to date emu I am aware of in terms of steam updates and interfaces, so I recommend to use it as well.

Though sometimes you'll see mp games listed, we either use online-fix.me or our own uc-online / uc-online2. Sometimes we'll use Unsteam, sometimes we'll get a game that works online even with gbe_fork applied. And every once in a while we'll just crack it ourselves so nothing has to be used.

As for drm cracks and other similar things, we usually find the crack-only files on cs.rin.ru and apply them to our own game files.

We do not deal with demo bypasses with Denuvo, and we are very on-the-fence with hypervisor stuff from DenuvOwO, just because of the risks that our users will have to take. It just depends on the game really.
