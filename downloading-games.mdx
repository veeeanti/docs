# Downloading games to upload

The team prefers Clean Steam Files (CSF) uploaded by cs.rin.ru members, files pulled with Depot Downloader or Accela, files from games you already own, or fresh purchases.

The team does not reupload other sites' releases — that's cheap and not what UnionCrax does. FitGirl, MyAbandonware, and standalone scene/crack releases are the only exceptions.

## Sources

- **cs.rin.ru** — Clean Steam Files shared by community members.
- **Depot Downloader** — Pulls files directly from Steam using app, depot, and manifest IDs.
- **Accela** — Generation-based Steam download service. See the [tools page](/tools-we-use) for the team's daily quota.
- **GOG** — DRM-free downloads. Always check here first.
- **ROMHEAVEN** — ROMs and CSF for older platforms.

<Note>
Always search cs.rin.ru thoroughly before assuming a game isn't already available.
</Note>

For step-by-step instructions per source, see [Getting started](/getting-started).
